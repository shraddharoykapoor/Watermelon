Watermelon

About Watermelons

Watermelons are large, juicy fruits loved worldwide for their refreshing taste. They belong to the Cucurbitaceae family and thrive in warm climates.

Popular Varieties

There are many types of watermelons including Seedless, Picnic, Yellow Flesh, and Mini watermelons. Each offers a unique taste and appearance.

Health Benefits

Watermelons are packed with vitamins A, C, and antioxidants like lycopene. They help keep you hydrated and may support heart health.
